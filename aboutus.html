About Hamro School

Hamro School is a warm, safe, and joyful preschool where children begin their learning journey with confidence and happiness.

We believe in play-based learning that helps children grow socially, emotionally, and intellectually in a caring environment.

Our trained and loving teachers focus on nurturing creativity, curiosity, discipline, and strong moral values in every child.
